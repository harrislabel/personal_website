Build Your Board

Buttery Snowboards is the brainchild of a few stoney snowboard fanatics born and raised in New York. Matthew Kessler and Harris Label brought together their idea of the optimal board creating process with the best materials available today. We at Buttery understand that riding style varies greatly from boarder to boarder. We understand that steeziness comes in all forms of snowboarding whether it be backcountry riding, freeriding, freestyle, all mountain or park/ jibbing. That's why we have YOU tell US how you want to ride and what you want to do with your board. Buttery Boards will put together the board of your dreams, in shop and customized just for you.
